About Me

Hello, my name is Olya - I am a photographer from Ukraine, from sunny Odessa.

About my photo

I want to tell about people, about their history, who they are, how they laugh, how they love and enjoy life, show them sincere, alive with a bright gleam in their eyes, real. I want to tell about you through a photo. If this is a wedding, through the years, after reviewing the album, you were able to return to that day again and smile. A portrait shooting - to see yourself as beautiful, unique, as I see each person in the frame, love story - to show feelings through a frozen moment. And if it is a family shooting is to preserve the happy memories of parents with them children, which are growing up so fast.

Naturalness is my priority, because it is what gives life to the frame. A simple photograph that conveys feelings through light, play of color, composition and a bit of magic.

My photo is about people, about their life - about beauty. Let yourself be who you are.
